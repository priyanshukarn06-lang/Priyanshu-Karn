About

I am Priyanshu Karn, a visual storyteller from Janakpurdham, now working from Kathmandu. I started my journey in 2020 as a wedding photographer, where storytelling through real emotions shaped my perspective.

Over time, my interest naturally evolved toward documentary filmmaking and event documentation, pushing me to explore deeper, people-centric narratives rooted in cultural authenticity.

For me, documentary photography is more than capturing moments — it's about preserving human emotions, untold stories, and the silent details that often go unnoticed. I have devoted myself to documenting the vibrant cultural life of the Mithila region, from the quiet devotion of prayer to the explosive energy of festival celebrations.

Currently open to collaborative projects rooted in culture, stories, and real experiences.
